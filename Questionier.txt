Organization Questionnaire - Product Carbon Footprint 

This questionnaire consolidates organizational and product-level data collection for calculating the Product Carbon Footprint (PCF) of automotive products or electronic components using a cradle-to-gate boundary condition. It integrates Scope 1, 2, 3, and avoided emissions, covering GHG Protocol, ISO 14067, and industry best practices.
